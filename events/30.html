Rules

  • Best of 3, single elimination. A bronze match will be played between semifinals losers. Finals and bronze matches are best of 5.
  • We will be playing using lichess.org
  • We will be playing using a 5+3 Blitz format. This means that each side will have 5 minutes to play, and after each turn 3 seconds will be added to the clock.
To start a match, click on the "play with a friend" button. From there, select "Standard", "Real time", and then set the "Minutes per side" to 5 and the "Increment in seconds" to 3. From there, click the white or black buttons to select your side (or the middle to make it random) to start the match. Send the link provided to your opponent to allow them to join.

To report the results of your matches, post in the tournament submissions channel. Questions can go in the chess tournament channel.

Scoring

1st place will be awarded 200 points
2nd place will be awarded 150 points
3rd place will be awarded 100 points
4th place will be awarded 50 points
5th place will be awarded 25 points
